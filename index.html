    About Us

    We are a team of specialized lawyers. For many years we have been advising and representing our clients in all legal matters of business life as well as in private law matters. Competent, solution-oriented and personal service is important to us. We develop efficient and pragmatic solutions for our clients’ concerns. Our clients benefit from our legal knowledge, our understanding of business matters and many years of experience.

    Furthermore, we have a proven network with contacts to highly qualified lawyers and other specialists in Europe, North and South America and Asia. We are a founding member of Eurolegal (eurolegal.net), a global association of currently 21 law firms with over 600 lawyers.
